Flower Tips & Guides🥀

How to Keep Flowers Fresh 🌹

Fresh flowers last longer if kept in clean water and stems are trimmed daily.

Change the water every day, re-cut stems at 45°, keep away from direct sunlight & ripening fruits to avoid ethylene exposure.

Flower Colors & Meanings 💐

Red = love, Yellow = friendship, White = purity, Purple = admiration.

Pink shows gratitude, orange for enthusiasm, and blue tones bring calm elegance to contemporary bouquets.
